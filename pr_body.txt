This PR adds oracle tests for various language extensions to track parser progress and compatibility.

Extensions covered:
- DerivingVia, EmptyDataDeriving, MagicHash, ExtendedLiterals
- PartialTypeSignatures, QualifiedDo, RequiredTypeArguments
- UnboxedTuples, UnboxedSums, TypeData, TypeFamilies
- RecordWildCards, RecursiveDo, ImplicitParams
- ForeignFunctionInterface, Arrows
